Wings Session

Nubs-Uenr has six wings. Please you are advise to join at least one wing. These include;

Outreach Wing

The Outreach Wing consist of JNUBS, which is nubs for Senior High Schools
The Outreach Wing is in charge of evangelism and charity

Theatre Wing

The Theatre wing is in charge of the Drama and Choreography department.
So if you are good in dancing and acting and also want to learn. You can join us.

Organising Wing

The Organising Wing consist of Ushering, Publicity and Technical department.
We also make fliers, sound and video editing. If you are interested, join us.

Music Wing

The Music Wing is in charge of the melodious songs in the church.
They also have a choir called; God's Love Choir. If love to sing, you can join us.

Bible Studies Wing

The Bible Studies Wing is in charge of Tuesday's Bible Studies.
They are have Quiz boys, who normally represent the church when they have Bible Quiz. If you want to know of the word, join us.

Prayer Wing

The Prayer Wing is in charge of Thursday's Prayers on the School Field.
They also have intercessors who stand in the gap to Prayer for the church and their families. If you love to pray, join us.
